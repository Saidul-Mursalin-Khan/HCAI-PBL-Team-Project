A guided, five-step journey

Teaching a computer to know when to ask a human for help

We build a news-sorting helper that answers the easy questions itself and hands the tricky ones to a human expert — and learns to do it while bothering the expert as little as possible. Follow the steps in order; each one unlocks the next.
