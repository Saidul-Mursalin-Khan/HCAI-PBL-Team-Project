Show me the technical details

A small logistic-regression gate is trained to defer exactly when doing so flips a wrong AI answer into a right one. Its decision threshold is calibrated on a held-out split to maximise combined accuracy. We judge it on deferral quality, not just accuracy: how often it defers, and how it does on the kept vs. deferred stories.
